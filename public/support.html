About Scout

Scout helps you discover movies and TV shows. Search by title, actor, or director, and see where to stream content in every country.

Features

  • Search movies, TV shows, and people
  • Save favorites to your watchlist
  • See cast, crew, ratings, and overviews
  • Find streaming availability worldwide
  • Trending content updated weekly

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the data come from?
Scout uses TMDB (The Movie Database) for movie and TV show information, including streaming availability.

Do I need an account?
No account is needed. Your watchlist is stored locally on your device.

Is Scout free?
Yes, Scout is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.
